Task instructions

A hospital treatment letter has been sent to the injured person’s representative. Complete a check on DRS to see if they have replied to this letter.

If they have not replied to the letter, call the injured person's representative and find out if the injured person had hospital treatment.

Based on this, decide what ‘task outcome’ to choose below.

If the representative has said the injured person did receive hospital treatment:

  • go to claim options (opens in a new tab) and choose 'update claim details' and change 'HS hospital treatment' from No to Yes
  • return to this page and choose ‘Close task – Hospital treatment took place’

If the representative has said the injured person did not receive hospital treatment:

  • go to claim details (opens in a new tab) to review injuries: if injuries are not serious, go to claim options and choose 'Confirm HS treatment not needed'
  • return to this page and choose ‘Close task – Hospital treatment did not take place'
  • if injuries are serious, go to claim options and choose 'Send a letter', followed by 'CRU COMP SI IP'
  • return to this page and choose ‘Close task – Sent injured person hospital treatment confirmation letter'

If the representative has not responded to the letter or call:

  • go to claim options (opens in a new tab) and choose 'Send a letter', followed by 'CRU COMP SI IP'
  • return to this page and choose ‘Close task – Sent injured person hospital treatment confirmation letter'
